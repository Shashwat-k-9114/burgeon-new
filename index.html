How we work

From concept to
commissioning

Every project follows a rigorous process — engineered for zero surprises on your production floor.

01
Consult & Design
Our engineers assess your part geometry, production volume, and space constraints to design the optimal system. No overengineering, no waste.
02
Fabricate
Everything is fabricated in our 25,000 sq ft IMT Manesar facility — CNC cutting, press braking, MIG/TIG welding — all in-house with strict quality control.
03
Install & Commission
Our team handles full site installation, PLC integration, and commissioning. We do not leave until the system runs flawlessly at production speed.
04
Support & AMC
Long-term Annual Maintenance Contracts and rapid-response support keep your production running at peak performance, year after year.
